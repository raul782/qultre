Last Updated: June 17, 2026

At Qultre (available at Qultre.com), we care deeply about user privacy. This policy details how we collect, use, and secure the data collected during the validation and waitlist stage of our platform.

## Information We Collect

When you sign up to register interest in the Qultre waitlist, we collect:
- **Name**: To personalize our communication with you.
- **Email Address**: To send you early beta invitations and updates regarding product features.
- **Preferred Language**: To ensure we communicate in your chosen language.
- **City Preference (Optional)**: To help us prioritize which cities and districts to release first.

## How We Use Your Data

We collect and process your email and details solely for the purpose of market validation, shipping invitations to the closed beta, and sending progress reports. Your data is stored securely using automated spreadsheets via our Google Sheets pipeline and is never shared, leased, or sold to third parties.

## Cookies & Local Storage

We use local browser storage (`localStorage`) on your device solely to remember your UI preference (Light or Dark theme) and ensure a seamless layout rendering during subsequent visits.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or would like to request the deletion of your data from the waitlist, please email us at `contact@qultre.com`.

Qultre was born out of the vision of a group of cultural researchers, interactive designers, and software engineers passionate about breaking down the invisible barriers of local knowledge.

We are a team with several decades of experience building scalable applications from the media and finance industry. Our multidisciplinary group includes software architects, distributed systems developers, and anthropologists dedicated to structured cultural discovery.
